FnF

(Footie & Fitness)

Alongside engaging group sessions, at Fitness with Champions we also offer tailored sessions aimed at improving individual players.

Under 15s Sessions

Ball work, fitness, strength & conditioning for developing children, rondo, in game play scenarios, defending, attacking, communication skills, scanning the pitch for space as well as many other enhancing activities.

Over 16s Sessions

Strength & conditioning, Tabata sprints, interval training, interval sprints, incline sprints, ball work under high pressure, breaking down a low block, crosses, finishes, off the ball movement, creating space, tracking runners as well as many more activities to develop onto that higher stage.
